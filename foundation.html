Our Story

Two practitioners who saw the same gap

Ken Johnston and Bob Rapp spent decades inside the machine — leading data, analytics, and AI programs at Microsoft, Ford, IBM Watson, GE Healthcare, Vodafone, and General Motors. They'd each sat in the room where the compliance slide was presented, watched it get noted, and seen nothing change in the pipeline before launch.

The pattern repeated across every organization: governance existed as intention, not infrastructure. Policies lived in PDFs. Audits happened after the fact. The gap between what the ethics framework said and what the system actually did grew wider with every deployment cycle.

The AIGovOps Foundation was built on a single conviction: governance must be treated like engineering. Versioned. Tested. Observable in production. Auditable by default. Not a checklist you run before launch — a system that ships alongside the product.

Our Mission

From Intentions to Evidence

"The AiGovOps Foundation focuses on creating a future where AI products contribute positively to humanity. Through rigorous audit, oversight, ethical frameworks as automated code, we ensure technology remains transparent and compliant with human values."

What AI GovOps Means

AI GovOps is governance that runs like engineering. It is the discipline of automating every element of safe, reliable, and compliant AI — from design through deployment, through operations, and through sunsetting. That means governance versioned like code, tested before deployment, observable in production, and auditable by default.

Why Now

Every AI system deployed without automated governance creates technical debt that compounds. Every compliance framework that exists only as a PDF is a liability waiting to surface. As regulatory requirements accelerate globally and AI scales into critical systems, the gap between governance intent and operational reality is widening — not closing. Organizations that embed governance into their deployment pipelines now will lead. Those that don't will spend the next decade retrofitting.

Core Pillars

Four disciplines. One operational system.

Pillar 01

Governance as Code

Embedding ethical frameworks and compliance checks directly into production pipelines as automated, testable code — so governance ships with the product, not after it.

Pillar 02

AI Technical Debt Elimination

Closing the gap between governance policy on paper and governance reality in production systems — identifying and resolving the silent liabilities that compound with every deployment.

Pillar 03

Operational Compliance

Automating regulatory alignment across jurisdictions so compliance scales with deployment, not against it — turning regulatory requirements into executable, auditable workflows.

Pillar 04

Community-Driven Standards

Building practitioner-led tools, practices, and open standards through a cross-functional community — because governance that works in production has to be built by the people who ship to production.

How This Project Embodies Our Mission

ai-bob-setup-agent is governance as code, running in production.

The foundation advocates for principles. This project lives them. Every automation decision, every pipeline gate, every monitoring hook in this repo is a concrete implementation of what "governance as code" looks like at the solo-operator scale.

YES · Ship AI

Onboarding Automation

Pre-flight checks and config validation ensure every customer environment meets governance standards before a single agent goes live. Governance ships with the product — not in a PDF reviewed after launch.

YES · Steady AI

Monitoring + Health Digests

The watchdog and daily health digests provide the continuous observability the foundation champions. Evidence over intention: if the system is compliant, the data shows it. If it isn't, the alert fires before the customer feels it.

YES · Recover AI

Watchdog Alerts + Auto-Recovery

Missed heartbeats trigger Telegram and email alerts. The system detects drift from expected behavior and surfaces it immediately — turning audit from a quarterly event into a continuous operational signal.

  • ✓ The CI/CD pipeline enforces quality gates — no code reaches production without passing automated governance checks.
  • ✓ Config is versioned like code. Every deployment is reproducible, auditable, and inspectable.
  • ✓ The open-source approach lets the community inspect, audit, and contribute to the governance automation — Community-Driven Standards in practice.
  • ✓ The observability stack (watchdog, health digests, Telegram alerts) provides the "evidence over intention" the foundation champions as core to operational compliance.
