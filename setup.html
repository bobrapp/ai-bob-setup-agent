Get Telegram Chat IDs

So the bot knows where to send notifications

  1. Send any message to your new bot in Telegram
  2. Open: https://api.telegram.org/bot[TOKEN]/getUpdates
  3. Find "chat":{"id": YOUR_NUMBER}
